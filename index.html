The Problem

Retail investors consistently underperform professional benchmarks

Retail investors fall behind not just because they lack data, but due to predictable behavioral mistakes. As a result, most underperform broad indices on a risk-adjusted basis, making trades that systematically move in the wrong direction. Understanding these patterns is key to smarter, science-driven investing.

Under-diversification

Holding too few stocks at any point in time.

Overconfidence

Overtrading leading to high transaction costs.

Disposition Bias

Holding losers too long, selling winners too early.

Lottery Stock Preference

Preference for speculative, long-shot stock bets.
